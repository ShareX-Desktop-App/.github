## 🚀 Overview
ShareX for Windows is a free capture app that packs a whole workflow into one lightweight tool. It works like a more capable snipping tool — grab a region, window, or full screen with a hotkey — and adds full screen recording to video or GIF plus scrolling capture for long pages. Once you've captured something, the built-in editor lets you annotate, blur, and refine it, while custom workflows automate the steps you take every time. A single ShareX download equips you to create, edit, and share screenshots and recordings without juggling multiple programs.

## ✨ Features
- **Screenshot Capture** — Grab a region, window, or the entire screen with your own keyboard shortcuts.
- **Screen Recording** — Record your display to video or GIF for tutorials, demos, and reports.
- **Scrolling Capture** — Stitch a long, scrolling page into one complete image.
- **Image Editor** — Annotate, blur, highlight, and add shapes or text before saving.
- **Automated Workflows** — Combine capture, edit, and export actions into one-press routines.
- **Extra Utilities** — Includes a color picker, ruler, OCR, and more built-in tools.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open ShareX — it sits in your system tray and stays ready in the background.
2. Choose your capture method: region, window, full screen, or screen recording.
3. Press your hotkey to take a screenshot or start recording your screen.

5. Save it to your PC or copy it to the clipboard, then share it however you need.

## ❓ FAQ
**What is ShareX?**
ShareX is a free, open-source screen capture and recording tool for Windows. It combines screenshots, screen recording, scrolling capture, an image editor, and workflow automation in one lightweight app.

**Is ShareX free?**
Yes. ShareX is completely free and open source. Every capture, recording, and editing feature is included at no cost, with no subscription required.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
ShareX runs on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping your system up to date helps captures and recordings run smoothly.

**Is my data secure?**
Your screenshots and recordings are saved locally on your own computer, and nothing is uploaded unless you choose to share it. You stay in full control of your captures.
